Donation FAQ

How will my donation be used?

Your donation will be used to provide immediate relief to disaster-affected communities, including emergency shelter, food, clean water, medical care, and other essential services. You can designate your donation to a specific relief effort or allow us to allocate it where it's needed most.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes, Relief Tracker is a registered 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization, and all donations are tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. You will receive a receipt via email for your records after making your donation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover), PayPal, bank transfers, and cryptocurrency donations. All payment methods are processed securely through our encrypted payment gateway.

Can I make a recurring donation?

Yes! During the donation process, you'll have the option to make your donation recurring (monthly, quarterly, or annually). Recurring donations help us plan for long-term recovery efforts and provide sustained support to affected communities.

How can I get my company to match my donation?

Many companies offer matching gift programs that can double or even triple your donation. After making your donation, check with your employer's HR department about their matching gift policy. We'd be happy to provide any documentation needed to process your matching gift.

How can I update my donation information?

You can update your donation information at any time by contacting our donor support team at donorsupport@relieftracker.org or by calling (800) 555-1234. Please have your donation reference number ready for faster service.
