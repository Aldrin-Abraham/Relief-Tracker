About Relief Tracker

Connecting communities with responders for faster, more effective disaster relief

Our Story

Founded in 2020 during the height of the global pandemic, Relief Tracker began as a small team of emergency responders and technologists who saw firsthand how disconnected relief efforts could be during large-scale disasters. After witnessing the chaos and inefficiencies in disaster response systems, we set out to create a better way.

What started as a simple idea to track medical supplies has grown into a comprehensive disaster management platform used by organizations in over 15 countries. Our system has been deployed in hurricanes, wildfires, earthquakes, and humanitarian crises, helping coordinate the delivery of aid to millions of affected individuals.

Our Mission

At Relief Tracker, we believe that technology should serve humanity, especially in our most vulnerable moments. Our mission is to bridge the gap between affected communities and relief providers through real-time information sharing and coordination tools.

Reduce Response Times

Cut critical response times by up to 60% through real-time alerts and optimized routing.

Save Lives

Ensure life-saving resources reach those who need them most when every second counts.

Empower Communities

Give affected individuals the tools to communicate their needs directly to responders.
