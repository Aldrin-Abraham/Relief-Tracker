About Relief Tracker

Founded in 2025, Relief Tracker is a disaster management platform dedicated to connecting resources with those in need during humanitarian crises. Our technology bridges the gap between relief organizations, volunteers, and affected communities.

"To revolutionize disaster response through real-time coordination, ensuring no call for help goes unanswered when every second counts."

How We Work

Relief Tracker combines cutting-edge geospatial technology with a network of verified responders to:

  • Pinpoint disaster-affected areas in real-time
  • Match resources with specific community needs
  • Coordinate multi-agency response efforts
  • Provide transparent tracking of aid distribution
